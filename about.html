my story

Hey there! I'm a half-full-stack developer who believes that great software is where art meets engineering. My journey began with a love for gaming and evolved into a passion for creating immersive digital experiences. Inspired by the epic storytelling of the Marvel Universe and the technical precision required in software development, I strive to build applications that are not only functional but also visually stunning and engaging. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new trends in technology and design, experimenting with UI/UX, and staying up to date with the latest in digital creativity. As a dedicated gamer, I bring the same curiosity, strategic thinking, and attention to detail from gaming into every project I tackle. I enjoy creating content for my YouTube channel, Anas Hany, where I share insights, tutorials, and tips to help others enhance their digital skills. My approach blends creativity, problem-solving, and technical knowledge, aiming to deliver experiences that are both meaningful and captivating. Curious, persistent, and constantly learning, I love taking on projects that push boundaries and allow me to explore new technologies and interactive storytelling. I believe that every line of code and design choice can contribute to an experience that’s both memorable and engaging.
